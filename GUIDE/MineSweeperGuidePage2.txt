How to Begin:
Select the "Create an Account" option from the main menu.
Enter the required information (username and password).
Once your account is created, you can log in and start playing!

Control Keys:
W: Move Up
A: Move Left
S: Move Down
D: Move Right
Enter: Select a cell to open
F: Flag a cell (mark it as a potential mine)
Esc: Return to the main menu

How to Play:

-Selecting Cells:
Use the WASD keys to move your cursor around the game board. Pressing these keys will move your position in the respective direction.
Once the cursor is on the cell you want to open, press Enter to reveal the contents of that cell.
